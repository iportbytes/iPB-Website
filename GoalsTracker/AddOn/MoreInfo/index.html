AddOn Instructions:

Open the ‘Goals Tracker’ Add On and enable the ‘Daily Trigger’ switch to let the ‘Goals Tracker’ take care of creating a daily sheet at the start of the day by duplicating the last day’s sheet. It also takes care of updating the Progress sheet by taking all the values from all of your daily sheets. Use the ‘Sync’ button in the ‘Goals Tracker’ Add On to update the Progress sheet, if you don’t want to wait till the end of the day.
